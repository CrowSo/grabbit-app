About SoundCloud downloads

SoundCloud is one of the most popular platforms for independent music, podcasts, and DJ mixes. While SoundCloud offers a paid "Go+" tier with offline listening in the app, there is no built-in way to save tracks as MP3 files to your PC for use outside the SoundCloud ecosystem. Grabbit fills this gap by using yt-dlp — the same engine trusted by thousands of developers — to download publicly available SoundCloud tracks as high-quality MP3 files.

Note: Grabbit only downloads public SoundCloud tracks. Tracks that are set to private or restricted to paid-only plays require a SoundCloud account to access and cannot be downloaded by Grabbit.

How do I download SoundCloud songs to my computer?

Install Grabbit for Windows, copy the SoundCloud track URL (soundcloud.com/artist/track-name), paste it into Grabbit, click Fetch, then click Add to Queue. The audio is saved as MP3 to your downloads folder.

No. Grabbit downloads public SoundCloud tracks only. Tracks set to private or restricted to SoundCloud Go+ subscribers are not accessible without authentication, which Grabbit does not support.

What format does Grabbit use for SoundCloud downloads?
