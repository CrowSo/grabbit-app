Step-by-Step: How to Download YouTube Videos with Grabbit

  1. 1
    Download and install Grabbit

    Go to appgrabbit.com and click "Download free — Windows". Run the GrabbitSetup.exe file and follow the installer. It takes about 30 seconds.

  2. 2
    Open Grabbit and paste your YouTube URL

    Launch Grabbit. Go to YouTube, copy the video URL from your browser, and paste it into the Grabbit input field. Click Fetch.

  3. 3
    Choose your format

    Select Video + Audio for an MP4 file, or Audio only to download as MP3. You can also choose Video only if you need it without audio.

  4. 4
    Choose quality

    Pick from available qualities. Free users get up to 720p. Pro users get up to 4K / 8K depending on what YouTube offers for that video.

  5. 5
    Download

    Click Add to Queue, then switch to the Queue tab and click Download. The file saves to your PC folder. Done.

How to Download YouTube Videos as MP3 on Windows

In step 3 above, select Audio only instead of Video + Audio. Grabbit will extract the audio track and save it as an MP3 file to your PC. This works for music videos, podcasts uploaded to YouTube, or any video where you only need the audio.
