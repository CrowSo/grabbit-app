Frequently asked questions

How do I download Facebook videos to my computer?

Install Grabbit for Windows, copy the Facebook video URL, paste it into Grabbit and click Fetch, then select Video+Audio and click Add to Queue. The video is saved to your chosen downloads folder in HD quality.

Can I download private Facebook videos?

No. Grabbit only downloads from public Facebook posts. Videos from private profiles, friends-only posts, or private groups are not accessible without account authentication, which Grabbit does not support.

Can I download Facebook Reels with Grabbit?

Yes. Facebook Reels are short video posts and work the same as any other Facebook video in Grabbit. Copy the Reel URL and paste it into Grabbit to download.

What format does Grabbit save Facebook videos in?
