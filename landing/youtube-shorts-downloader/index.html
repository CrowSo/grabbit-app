YouTube Shorts vs regular YouTube videos — what's different for downloading?

YouTube Shorts are short-form vertical videos (under 60 seconds) that use a different URL format: youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID instead of the usual youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID. The content is still hosted on YouTube's servers — it's the same infrastructure, just a different URL path and player interface.

Grabbit treats Shorts exactly like any other YouTube video. When you paste a Shorts URL, Grabbit recognizes it automatically and downloads it in full quality. The Chrome Extension also works on Shorts pages — click the Grab button while watching the Short to send it to Grabbit with one click.
