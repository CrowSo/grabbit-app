About Pinterest video downloads

Pinterest hosts millions of video pins — tutorials, recipes, DIY projects, product showcases, and more. The problem with web-based Pinterest video downloaders is that they're loaded with pop-up ads and deceptive "Download" buttons that lead to malware. Grabbit solves this by being a proper desktop application: paste the link, click download, the file is on your PC. No redirects, no browser popups.

Pinterest uses both full URLs (pinterest.com/pin/123456...) and short links (pin.it/AbCdEf). Many web tools break on the short format because they don't follow redirects. Grabbit handles both transparently — just paste whatever URL you copied.
