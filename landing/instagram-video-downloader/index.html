What content can Grabbit download from Instagram?

Grabbit can download video content from public Instagram accounts. This includes Reels (the short-form vertical videos), standard feed posts with video, and IGTV long-form videos. The content must be publicly accessible — Grabbit does not support logging in to private accounts, as this would violate Instagram's terms of service.

Instagram GIFs and images from carousels are not the primary use case for Grabbit — it's optimized for video content. If you need to save still images from Instagram, your browser's right-click save feature is typically faster.
