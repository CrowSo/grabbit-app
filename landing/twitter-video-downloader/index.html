Quick answer

To download Twitter/X videos to your PC: install Grabbit for Windows, copy the tweet link (twitter.com or x.com), paste it into Grabbit, click Fetch, select Video+Audio, and click Add to Queue. The video saves directly to your computer in full quality — no cloud, no account needed.

How to download Twitter/X videos to your computer — step by step

  • 1
    Download Grabbit for Windows — free installer, under 80 MB. Works on Windows 10 and 11.

    Copy the tweet URL — on Twitter or X, click the Share icon on the post and select Copy link to post. Both twitter.com and x.com URLs work.
  • 3

Frequently asked questions

How do I download Twitter/X videos to my computer?

Install Grabbit for Windows, copy the tweet URL from twitter.com or x.com, paste it into Grabbit, click Fetch, choose Video+Audio and click Add to Queue. The video saves to your PC — no browser extension needed, though the optional Chrome extension also works for quick one-click grabs.

Does Grabbit work with the new x.com domain?

Yes. Grabbit handles both twitter.com and x.com URLs. When Twitter rebranded to X and changed its domain, Grabbit's underlying engine (yt-dlp) was updated to support both. No changes needed on your end.

What quality do Twitter videos download in?

Twitter serves videos at multiple resolutions depending on what was uploaded — typically up to 1080p. Grabbit automatically selects the best available quality. For some videos Twitter may limit the maximum resolution.
